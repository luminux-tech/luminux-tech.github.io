Who we are

A little bit about us...

Startup venture company started by an IT company, AgroMukam will be the first founded B2C e-commerce platform in South Asia for the fisheries, livestock and agricultural industry for the farmers, business organizations, veterinarians and manufacturers in the industry who want the best of the best of products from the market in just a click.

AgroMukam will offers diverse assortments in all categories of fisheries, livestock and agricultural categories ranging from every day farmers and commercial organization needs and their supplies including industrial manufacturing requirements.

AgroMukam will commit to provide an excellent customer experience, simplified purchase, solution oriented customer care and a hassle free purchasing and return experience with the best delivery channels.
