The Ponds View

Handshake distance with The Ponds

The Ponds View offers the perfect location in North Western Sydney's Heartland Schofields, a unique brand new residential community surrounded by the prestigious suburb The Ponds.

Local School and Great Community

The project is just opposite to the new The Ponds High School and just 3 minutes from Rouse Hill Town Centre. The Ponds View is situated in the most popular Alex Avenue Precinct of Schofields.
