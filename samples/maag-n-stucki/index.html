Log Book Servicing

A log book service is a broad service that is guided by the vehicle manufacturer’s specifications. Completing these services helps to preserve your vehicle’s statutory warranty. The log book itself contains all information for your vehicle’s service requirements for around the first 200,000km. It will let your mechanic know exactly what needs to be serviced at that specific service interval.
